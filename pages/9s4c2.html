⚠️ Important: There is no special rule for third-person singular pronouns (he/jí/él, she/shí/ella, it/it/ello). They follow the same structure as all pronouns (I/ái/yo, you/yú/tú/usted, we/uí/nosotros, they/déi/ellos).
