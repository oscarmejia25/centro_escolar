📚 Class 2: Verb Be

Negative Forms & Contractions / Formas Negativas y Contracciones

🎯 Learning Objective / Objetivo de Aprendizaje

Aprenderemos a usar el verbo "to be" en su forma negativa con contracciones. Al final de la clase podrás decir lo que las personas NO SON de manera natural.

📖 Grammar Explanation / Explicación Gramatical

¿Qué son las contracciones negativas?

Son formas acortadas que combinan el verbo "to be" con "not". En lugar de decir "I am not", decimos "I'm not". [[1]]

💡 Regla importante: Las contracciones negativas se forman de dos maneras:
  • Forma 1: verbo + n't → isn't, aren't
  • Forma 2: verbo contraído + not → he's not, they're not

📝 Part II (Class 2) - WORKSHEET

Instructions: Write the simple present of the verb "be" in the negative form. Use contractions.
(Escribe el presente simple del verbo "be" en forma negativa. Usa contracciones)

1. Carlos works at an office. He __________ a doctor.

2. Maria and I bake cakes and cook delicious food. We __________ police officers.

3. I work at a school. I __________ a firefighter.

4. Samuel and Valeria work at the police station. They __________ astronauts.

5. My mom works at the bank. She __________ a taxi driver.

6. You work at the restaurant. You __________ an optician.

7. Carolina works in the courtroom. She __________ a firefighter.

8. We work at the fire station. We __________ pilots.

Answer Key / Clave de Respuestas:

  1. He isn't a doctor. (o he's not)
  2. We aren't police officers. (o we're not)
  3. I 'm not a firefighter.
  4. They aren't astronauts. (o they're not)
  5. She isn't a taxi driver. (o she's not)
  6. You aren't an optician. (o you're not)
  7. She isn't a firefighter. (o she's not)
  8. We aren't pilots. (o we're not)

✨ Summary / Resumen

Key Points / Puntos Clave:

  • I am not → I'm not (única forma)
  • He/She/It is not → isn't o he's not/she's not/it's not
  • You/We/They are not → aren't o you're not/we're not/they're not
  • El apóstrofe (') reemplaza las letras que se eliminan
  • Ambas formas son correctas: "isn't" y "he's not" [[2]]
